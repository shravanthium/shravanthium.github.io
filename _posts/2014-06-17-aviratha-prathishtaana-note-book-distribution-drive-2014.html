"A journey of thousand miles begins with a single step".

Aviratha is a vibrant youth force formed to serve humanity and mother land. Aviratha group is a confluence of people of all walks of life, from artists to literary laureates, from techies to scientists, working for a logical growth of the motherland.Yearly Notebook Drive, Career Guidance, Rural Health camps, Computer Workshops, Kannada Plays, Musical Evenings are some of the activities organized by the team Aviratha.

Annual Notebook Distribution Drive has been a phenomenal program conducted by Aviratha volunteers to the benefit of economically weaker students in rural Karnataka. This event is unique in itself, ensuring that every child receives the required quantity and variety of notebooks for one full academic year.
